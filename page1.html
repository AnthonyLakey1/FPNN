How Sleeping Can Reduce Calories!

Author

Anthony Lakey

Published

November 18, 2022

You know that all nighter you're about to go pull? Well, you may just want to go to sleep because news studies have found saying that a good night sleep can actually help reduce the amount of calories in your body. And even help you lose a little bit of weight. All you have to do is sleep.

Everyday, millions of Americans indulge in fast food restaurants. These are known for their high calorie menu.

The average person burns fifty (50) calories per hour by sleep. Sleeping for a standard eight (8) hours could be four-hundred (400) calories burnt. The average person is suppose to consume two-thousand and five-hundred (2,500) calories per day.

Many resort to lifting and other exercise to burn fat, which is produced from high calorie intake. Other methods are eating healthier, which still adds calories.

So go to sleep tonight and lose some unwanted calories!
